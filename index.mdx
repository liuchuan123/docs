## Get started in three steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Sign up at the PayMatrix dashboard and complete merchant onboarding to activate your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Grab your API keys">
    Navigate to **Settings → API Keys** to find your test and live keys. Start with the test key to build your integration.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Make your first payment">
    Call `POST /payments` with your key in the `Authorization` header. See the [Quickstart](/quickstart) for a complete walkthrough.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Start in the [sandbox environment](/integration/testing) — all test transactions are free and no real money moves. Switch to your live API key when you're ready to go live.
</Tip>
