PayMatrix is a unified payment processing platform built for modern businesses that need to accept, manage, and reconcile payments at scale. Whether you're running an e-commerce store, a SaaS subscription service, or a marketplace, PayMatrix gives you a single REST API to handle every stage of the payment lifecycle — from the moment a customer checks out to the moment funds land in your bank account.

## What you can do with PayMatrix

PayMatrix is designed for developers and finance teams alike. With one integration, you get the infrastructure to run payments across channels, currencies, and use cases — without stitching together multiple vendors.

  <Card title="Multi-channel acceptance" icon="credit-card">

  </Card>
  <Card title="Multi-currency support" icon="globe">

  </Card>
  <Card title="Real-time tracking" icon="radar">

  </Card>
  <Card title="Automated settlements" icon="building-columns">

  <Card title="Webhooks" icon="webhook">
